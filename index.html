Professional Profile

Computational Biologist and Data Scientist with a PhD in Biomedicine. Brings 5 years of experience building statistical models to extract insights from genomic, clinical, and other data types, supporting research and business decisions. Curious and organized, thrives on innovative projects, working independently or in collaborative teams.

Professional Experience

  • Analyzed genomic data from 60,000 cancer patients across 20 cancer types to quantify driver mutations and selection patterns.
  • Developed modular R framework with GLMs, Bayesian inference, autoencoders, improving driver detection in panel sequencing data.
  • Extended framework for timing/conditional selection analyses across datasets (targeted panels/WES).
  • Managed 4-year project end-to-end, delivering/defending PhD thesis excellent cum laude with 50 advanced visualizations.
  • Created reports/presentations for supervisors, collaborators, funders, evaluators, conferences.

  • Collaborated on development of Bayesian models to infer cancer evolution and mutagenesis.
  • Co-authored high-impact research paper on clinical impact of copy number alterations in cancer genes (medRxiv preprint).

  • Computational Chemistry Group (02/2021–06/2021): Modeled prion proteins using 3D structure prediction (Rosetta).
  • Prion Lab (09/2020–12/2020): Conducted prion experiments as neurodegenerative disease model.

Performed genetic material extraction, Sanger sequencing, genotyping, phylogeny of Rotavirus A (RVA) from 100 wild animal samples. Master\'s thesis resulted in two co-authored publications, each cited >10 times.

Conducted diagnostics and genotyping of HIV & Hepatitis A from blood samples in the diagnostics department.
